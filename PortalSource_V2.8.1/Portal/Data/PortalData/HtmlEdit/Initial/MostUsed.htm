ContentScheduler
With this module you can place your content on a webpage. It allows to switch the content based on your defined schedule.
You can use this to have allways recent content of your pages.

FileBrowser
With this module you can upload your files from your computer and share with other people.

FormBuilder
A module that provides the ability to build your own Web-Forms that will be submitted by email. You can configure your own email layout and the data validation rules.
You can use this to build forms like a contact form, a registration form...

Forum
With this module you can place a forum for discussions on your website.

Guestbook
With this module you can place a simple guestbook on your website.

HitCounter
If you place this module on your startpage, you can always see the number of your visitors.

HtmlEdit
With this module you can place your content on a webpage.

ImageBrowser
This is an image gallery. You can present your uploaded pictures (You need to upload the pictures by FTP or another tool) in a gallery. The gallery supports directorys, and supports the decoration of your pictures with a shadow.

Login
This is the Login panel which is used to authenticate a user.

MyUser
A user can change his own data in this module.

Newsticker
This module presents the data of an external RSS source. (i.e. http://sourceforge.net/export/rss2_projnews.php?group_id=71816)

QuickLinks 
An easy to use list of links.

Quotations
With this module you can randomly show a quotation (or a tip of the day) from a list.

Table
With this module you can place a simple table on your webpage.

TabList / TabTree
Those modules show you the sub-tabs of the current selected tab in a tree or a list.

Website
This module allow you to embed an external website in your portal.

 

You will find other modules with a self explaining name.
